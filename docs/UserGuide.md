# GUIDOC User Guide

Welcome to GUIDOC, your AI-powered platform for simplifying government document application processes. This guide will walk you through the core features of the platform.

## 1. Getting Started

- **Registration & Login**: Create an account using your email and password. Once logged in, you will be directed to your personalized dashboard.
- **Language Selection**: You can change the application language to Hindi using the language toggle in the navigation bar.

## 2. Navigating the Dashboard

Your Dashboard is the central hub. Here you can:
- View your **Saved/Favorite Documents**.
- Check the status of your ongoing applications via the **Application Tracker**.
- See notifications regarding upcoming appointments or document renewals.

## 3. Finding Documents

- **Search**: Use the global search bar on the home page or in the navigation menu to find documents by name (e.g., "Aadhaar Card", "Passport").
- **Categories**: Browse documents by categories such as Identity, Vehicle, Property, and Health.

## 4. Document Details

Clicking on a document will show you comprehensive information:
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Who can apply for this document.
- **Required Documents**: What you need to submit.
- **Online vs Offline Steps**: Detailed step-by-step instructions.
- **Fees & Time**: Estimated costs and processing times.

## 5. Eligibility Wizard

Not sure if you qualify?
1. Navigate to the **Eligibility Wizard**.
2. Select the document you wish to apply for.
3. Answer a few simple questions.
4. Get an instant YES/NO result along with reasoning.

## 6. AI Voice Assistant

Need quick answers?
- Click the **Voice Assistant** microphone icon.
- Speak your query naturally (e.g., "What are the fees for a Passport?").
- The assistant will process your voice, fetch the answer, and reply using speech.

## 7. Application Tracker

Keep track of your document statuses:
- Add a new application to track.
- Update its status (e.g., "Submitted", "In Progress", "Completed").
- View a timeline of your document journey.
